About MSO

The SKSU Muslim Student Organization (MSO) is dedicated to fostering unity, leadership, and spiritual growth among Muslim students. Our mission is to promote Islamic values, organize beneficial events, and build a supportive community within the campus and beyond.

We conduct religious, educational, and charitable programs while encouraging inclusiveness, mutual respect, and active student engagement.
